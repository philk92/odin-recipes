Spaghetti Bolognese

Recipe for a Risotto alla Philip, as used in his diet.

We all love Risotto. This one is good for eating in a diet.

Ingredients

  • 100g risotto risotto
  • 2 onions
  • 35ml white wine
  • 15g butter
  • 1000ml beef broth

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and cook them in the butter.
  2. When the onions start getting brown, add the rice and stir for a minute.
  3. Now its time for extinguish the onions and rice with the white wine.
  4. Start adding the beef broth bit by bit. After like 20 mins the risotto is done!

Bon appetit!
